Congratulations to **Team NEXUS (Emon, Phonesamay, Jinling Yu, Zhaojia Wang)** for standing out from over 350 teams nationwide, advancing to the finals, and winning the Excellence Award. The Lab member Wang Wei served as the problem setter for the "ONNX Runtime Operator Optimization" track in the Pioneer Cup, tasking participants with optimizing core operators such as Attention on Hygon DCU.

祝贺CogTwins实验室**NEXUS团队（Emon, Phonesamay, 于金令, 王曌嘉）**从全国350余支队伍中脱颖而出，晋级2025年先导杯智算决赛并荣获优胜奖。实验室成员王威担任了“先导杯”ONNX Runtime算子优化赛题出题人，要求参赛者基于海光DCU，对Attention等核心算子进行性能优化。
