To promote the efficient integration of laboratory research outcomes with frontline industry needs, Cogtwins Lab conducted a specialized technical exchange with Qingdao Tianrui Tech Group today. During the meeting, in-depth discussions were held with Tianrui Group Chairman Qu Tiangui and relevant enterprise representatives, resulting in the identification of multiple potential collaboration points.
为促进实验室研究成果与产业一线需求的高效对接，今日，Cogtwins实验室赴青岛田瑞科技集团进行专项技术交流，并与田瑞集团董事长曲田桂及及企业相关负责人深入探讨，交流后取得了诸多合作点。
