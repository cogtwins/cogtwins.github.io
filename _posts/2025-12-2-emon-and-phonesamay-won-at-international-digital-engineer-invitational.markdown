At the Industrial Internet track of the first Shandong Digital Engineers Innovation and Creativity Competition & International Digital Engineers Invitational, Cogtwins lab member **AYUB ALI EMON** and **PHOUTTHAVONG PHONESAMAY** won the first prize with their project **"IntelliFactory: A Multi-Agent Digital Twin Platform for Industrial Optimization"**, showcasing an innovative multi-agent digital twin platform for industrial optimization.

在首届山东省数字工程师创新创意大赛暨国际数字工程师邀请赛工业互联网赛道中，cogtwins实验室成员**AYUB ALI EMON**和**PHOUTTHAVONG PHONESAMAY**凭借作品**《IntelliFactory: A Multi-Agent Digital Twin Platform for Industrial Optimization》**荣获一等奖。该作品展示了面向工业优化的多智能体数字孪生平台创新成果。
