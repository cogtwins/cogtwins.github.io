A recent research-industry collaboration matching event was held between the research team from Shandong University of Science and Technology and Dongming Ji'an Agriculture and Animal Husbandry Co., Ltd. Cogtwins Lab conducted an on-site visit to the company's production processes and held in-depth discussions with General Manager Guo Anjun and relevant enterprise representatives. The exchange resulted in the identification of multiple potential areas for collaboration, laying a solid foundation for joint technological research.山东科技大学科研团队与东明吉安农牧有限公司的产研合作对接活动于近期举行。Cogtwins实验室实地考察了企业生产流程，并与公司总经理郭安军及技术骨干进行了深入交流。双方交流后取得了诸多合作点，为联合技术攻关奠定了坚实基础。
