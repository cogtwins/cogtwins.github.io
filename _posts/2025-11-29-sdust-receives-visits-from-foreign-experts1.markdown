On 23rd October, the School of Computer Science and Engineering hosted a visit from foreign experts hailing from multiple regions including Europe, Asia, North America, South America, and Oceania. Following the meeting,  Professor Fernanda Gusmão de Lima Kastensmidt from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul, Professor Enrique Herrera, Vice-Rector of the University of Granada, and Professor Zhou Jiehan from the CSE were interviewed by the university. They expressed strong willingness and firm confidence in further deepening international collaboration with Shandong University of Science and Technology and the School of Computer Science and Engineering.计算机学院10.23日接待了来自欧洲、亚洲、北美、南美、大洋洲等多个地区的外国专家来访。在交流活动结束后，南里奥格兰德州立联邦大学教授Fernanda Gusmão de Lima Kastensmidt、格拉纳达大学副校长Enrique Herrera教授以及山东科技大学计算机科学与工程学院周杰韩教授分别接受了学校采访。他们在采访中表达了与山东科技大学及计算机学院进一步深化国际合作的强烈意愿与坚定信心。
