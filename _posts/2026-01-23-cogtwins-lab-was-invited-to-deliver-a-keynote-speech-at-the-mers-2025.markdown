On January 23, 2026, the Cogtwins Lab was invited to deliver a keynote speech at the MERS 2025 at the University of Moratuwa in Sri Lanka. Via an online format, Prof. Zhou presented on the topic "Towards Industrial Agents with Embodied Robotics for Complex Manufacturing Systems," sharing cutting-edge explorations in the integration of industrial agents and embodied robotics.
2026年1月23日，Cogtwins实验室受邀于斯里兰卡莫勒图沃大学（Moratuwa University）MERS 2025研讨会发表主题演讲。通过线上方式，团队以“面向复杂制造系统的工业智能体”为题，分享了在工业智能体与具身机器人融合领域的前沿探索。
