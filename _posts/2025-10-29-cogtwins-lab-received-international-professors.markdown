The International Office and Cogtwins Lab hosted distinguished international professors from Australia, Canada, Spain, Brazil, Poland, Italy, Uruguay, Finland, and other countries. The visiting scholars successively toured the Advanced Manufacturing Innovation Center of the School of Mechatronics, the Mine Virtual Reality and Simulation Center, the International Office of the School of Computer Science, and the Cogtwins Lab. Numerous collaboration intentions were reached with the School of Computer Science and the Cogtwins Lab.今日，国际办公室与Cogtwins实验室接待国际专家教授。来自澳大利亚，加拿大，西班牙，巴西，波兰，意大利，乌拉圭，芬兰等国家的教授，先后访问了机电学院的先进制造创新中心，矿山虚拟现实与仿真中心，计算机国际办公室和Cogtwins实验室，与计算机学院和cogtwins实验室达成了众多的合作意向。
