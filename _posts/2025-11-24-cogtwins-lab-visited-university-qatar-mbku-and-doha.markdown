Cogtwins Lab visited Qatar University, MBKU, and the University of Doha on 23rd November, holding in-depth discussions with the Schools of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering. Both parties plan to establish international collaboration in cutting-edge fields such as advanced computing, new energy technologies, and autonomous driving, aiming to jointly promote scientific research innovation and talent exchanges. This visit has laid a solid foundation for future international, interdisciplinary cooperation and technological innovation.Cogtwins实验室代表团近日访问卡塔尔大学，MBKU和多哈大学，与计算机学院、电子工程学院展开深入交流。双方计划在先进计算、新能源技术、自动驾驶等前沿领域建立国际合作，共同推动科研创新与人才培养。此次访问为未来跨学科协作与技术创新奠定了国际合作基础。
